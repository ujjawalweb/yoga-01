What Is Kundalini?

Kundalini means coiling. It is described as a coiled serpent located at the base of your spine. Kundalini conveys the sense of never-ending potential energy. The energy runs along and upward along your spine up to your head.

How To Awaken Kundalini?

You can awaken the Kundalini through two approaches:

  • ● Shaktipat / Siddha Mahayoga / Kundalini Mahayoga / Sahaja Yoga.
  • ● Kriya Yoga (Laya Yoga), Hatha Yoga, and Mantra Yoga.

How To Become A Kundalini Yoga Teacher?

Anyone can become a Kundalini Yoga Teacher by completing the teacher training programs. You will get a certificate after the completion of the course.

You can use this certificate to register your membership with Yoga Alliance. They will give you an RYT certificate. After that, you will be able to teach anywhere in the world..

How Does Kundalini Differ From Other Forms?

Kundalini yoga incorporates the mental, physical, and spiritual aspects of yoga. It focuses on spirituality, Chanting, Hand Positions, Meditation, and Breathing to bring balance between your mind, body, and soul.

What Are The Important Aspects Of Kundalini Yoga Practice?

Here are the top 5 aspects of the practice:.

  • Breath Of Fire.
  • Mantras
  • Kriyas
  • Mudras
  • Kundalini Meditations.

Is It Safe To Awaken Your Kundalini?

Yes, The process is safe and gentle. You will have to train your mind and body to hold energy frequencies awakened by Kundalini.

The practices prepare your nervous system and body to receive potent energy. Practice regularly to live a conscious life.

How Much Time Does It Take In Kundalini Awakening?

There is no time frame. Every individual responds to these yoga exercises differently. There are few and rare cases of immediate kundalini awakening, and sometimes, it takes years of practice. The awakening process is gradual. In short, it is hard to find out the time frame for the occurrence.
