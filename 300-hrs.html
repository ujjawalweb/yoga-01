SYLLABUS For RYT 300 Yoga Teacher Training Rishikesh

Yoga History & Advanced Asana Study:

Learn how to teach advanced yoga poses, including teaching cues, modifications, injury considerations, directions, and demonstrations. Ancient & Different Styles

We will teach you in-depth knowledge of the Bhagavad Gita. Learn the different forms of yoga and how it relates to modern-day life. The course creates a basic understanding of the differences between different forms of yoga, such as Hatha yoga, Vinyasa yoga, Yin yoga, and Kundalini yoga

Tension & Compression:

We will also teach you how to identify compression or tension in graduates or students to help deliver safer guidance to students working through different meditation and asanas practices.

Ayurveda & Yoga Nidra:

Understand the principles of this ancient sister science of yoga & the 3 Doshas. You will explore healing techniques, diet plans, meditation practices, a yogic lifestyle, and various exercises. Advanced Teaching Techniques & Philosophy:

Learn advanced teaching techniques to help master your classroom. Learn how to teach beginner, intermediate, and advanced-level students. Get the best teaching techniques. Explore the philosophical principles and concepts of the Yoga Sutras. You will also learn how to apply yogic principles to your own life.

Pranayama & Meditation Techniques:

Discover different guided meditation techniques, including Kindness, Walking, Gazing, Mindfulness, etc. Learn advanced Pranayama techniques to understand better this practice can be incorporated into your classes and your practice.s

Creative & Inspiring Classes:

Our course will teach you how to teach your classes with creativity to uplift the curiosity of your students. Learn about basic yogic principles related to advanced class. Additionally, we will teach you how to set up a creative and inspiring class.

Yoga Ethics & Personal Development

Take a look into how ethics affect your success as a professional yoga teacher and various ways to incorporate the principles of yoga into your classroom.
